## Tặng cho cậu

## aquilliora

Vơn, tiếng Anh tui nô được hay, nhưng mờ tui phải viết sau khi nghe được có một bạn của tui đang bị sốc khi có một điều...không được tốt cho lắm xảy ra với người bạn của cậu ta. Những cảm xúc trong này là thật và tui hi vọng lờ ngày đẹp trời lào đó thì tui sẽ trans qua tiếng Việt được, bài văn này đã được một người bạn khác của tui dùng để làm hồi chuông cảnh tỉnh cho một người bạn khác đang có mâu thuẫn với cha mẹ mình :3 Nhân vật và sự kiện đã diễn ra ở ngoài đời thật sẽ không được nhắc đến, chủ yếu lờ tui nhắc tới chuyện tâm lí và chủ yếu chỉ lờ để an ủi, động viên thâu :3 (Tuy nhiên tui chưa có cơ hội để gửi cái này cho cậu bợn đó, tợi 1 lờ tui ngại >w<, 2 lờ tui nghĩ nếu giờ mà làm thế thì hông chừng bạn tui lại buồn nữa nên tốt nhất lờ cứ xả trên lày) :3

Ẹc, câu thời gian đủ rầu nhể? :3 Đoạn văn đây >w< :

This is for a friend of mine, I don’t know much about what happened, but losing someone important to you can make you feel really devastated and depressed, I don’t have the proper vocabulary to describe how I feel right now but this has made me realize how bad of a kid I am. I know I shouldn’t be treating people around me like this because…one day when you wake up, things might not be…the same again. As for you bro, crying is inevitable right now, so you can cry all you want, from the moment when tears start streaming down your face, you can be weak – but after that, you know they are important to you and you have to move on with them living within your heart. So smile to show them that you are happy and are lucky enough to have met them. Things are going to be alright. I write this to you because you might not be a friend I have in real life and I’m sure that you are a hell lot older than me, but I just can’t stand looking at a friend, whether to be real or not, break down because that would tear my heart apart.

_______________________________________________________​It’s funny how people can have so many feelings at once and life brings us different tastes of everything we can experience in life. That’s what makes our lives exciting and not boring, but at the same time, life is harsh and it can throw tons of challenges at us, and we have to deal with those. Losing someone is no exception. It’s your biggest challenge as God comes to take those who you loved to Heaven. How can you overcome your grief? It’s not something that can be overcome immediately and it’s definitely not something you can forget about. If they’re important to you, things might be worse when they’re gone, you might have suicidal thoughts and you might be really depressed. People who are your friends would go and offer you condolences for your loss – but it doesn’t really matter for you, as you’ll feel like you’re stuck and can’t get over the loss of your loved ones. I’m just like those people who give you condolences and it’s kind of one sided, as I do not know the whole story and what happened to them, so you might think that I’m just bringing that up to make you upset and I feel bad about it.

You can’t believe what you just heard was true and it breaks your heart, but as strong as you are, you’ll move on, everyone has to move on, but will the pain of losing someone important to you ever go away?  I can’t say yes, but for one thing I know, you will have to be strong and move on, but don’t fake it, you have to keep it real. When someone talks to you about it, don’t snap it off and get angry at them – you know what, it’s okay to do so but don’t let your grief have control over you. It takes time to get over people you’re way too fond of and especially if those people are your friend for a long time. It’s a challenge that life gives you, and after it, may you become a stronger man, just remember that they live within your heart and their souls are living a peaceful life on Heaven.

You have to move on. Be strong and enjoy life.
