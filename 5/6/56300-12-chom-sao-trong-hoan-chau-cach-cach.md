## 12 chom sao trong hoan chau cach cach

## thienthananhsang

ma ket vs song ngu: nhi khang va tu vi 
kim nguu vs bach duong: nhi thai va cong chua tay tang
xu nu vs thien yet: tinh nhi vs tieu kiem 
nhan ma vs su tu: tieu yen tu vs ngu a ca 
cu giai: ham huong
thien binh: lenh phi
bao binh: kim toa
song tu: hoang hau
