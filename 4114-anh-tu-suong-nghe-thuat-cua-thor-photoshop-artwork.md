1/ Toxic:

Toxic by Thor::.., on Flickr
Have you ever wonder, what you're gonna do next ? Because you love people a lot. Sometimes, that love is way too much , it hurts you inside.  But I believe, when time's passed, we all understand that love is never "too much" . Though love leads us to mistakes , it's still better than never striving for a true love. Is that right ?

2/ Magical Feather

Magical Feather by Thor::.., on Flickr
An artwork of mine.
Date: September 16th 2011
Photoshop CS5

Inspired from a dream of myself. Actually in my dream, I was trying to catch a flying apple. But I think if it were a feather, the image would be more dreamy.
Chỉ là một giấc mơ thôi !  Thực ra trong mơ, mình đang chuẩn bị hái một trái táo đỏ.
